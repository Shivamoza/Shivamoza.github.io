Project information

  • Project: Forecasting Stocks using Streamlit
  • Description: “ The motivated idea is that, if we know all information about today’s stock trading (of all specific traders), the price is predictable. Thus, if we can obtain just a partial information, we can expect to improve the current prediction lot”
  • Languages used: Streamlit, LSTM, FbProphet, Yahoo finance
  • Semester / Year: Third Year | Semester 5 & 6

All about Project

We have created a single-page web application using Streamlit and some machine learning models which will show company information (logo, registered name, and description) and stock plots based on the stock code given by the user. Also, we have used in yahoo finance, and Facebook Prophet for Advanced Data and Graphs. Stock market prediction and analysis are some of the most difficult jobs to complete. There are numerous causes for this, including market volatility and a variety of other dependent and independent variables that influence the value of a certain stock in the market. These variables make it extremely difficult for any stock market expert to anticipate the rise and fall of the market with great precision. However, with the introduction of Machine Learning and its strong algorithms, the most recent market research and Stock Market Prediction advancements have begun to include such approaches in analysing stock market data.
