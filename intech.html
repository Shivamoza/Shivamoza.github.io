All about certification

Intech is a National Level Poster cum Project Competition, platform for the engineering students to present their innovative ideas as working project.
Tha main objective of Intech are:
1. Promote project and application-oriented development activities.
2. Help young engineers to interact, exchange of information & innovative ideas.
3. INTECH-2k23 provides a platform for FY, SY, TY and LY undergraduate students to present their project work & innovative ideas in front of industry experts to get exposure for working with industrial atmosphere.
